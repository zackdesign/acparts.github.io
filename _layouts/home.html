04 — The story

Anthony Campbell.

{% if about.image.url %} {% endif %}
{% if about.content %} {{ about.content | markdownify }} {% else %}

After school I left Victoria with my P plates and beloved 1975 HJ Monaro for the Northern Territory — mining industry, hydraulic machinery, and a lot learned fast.

Home again, I worked for J&S Ladhams Contracting operating tractors, loaders, slurry tanks and silage gear, before landing at Pearson Ag in Colac. Six years as workshop and parts manager, fitting Pearson loaders and assembling Buckton wagons, Sam spreaders, Jaylor mixers, Quinn forklifts.

In 2004 Pearson closed the Australian workshop. I saw an opportunity to keep the parts and warranty service going independently, and AC Service and Parts has been supplying machinery dealers and customers across Australia ever since.

I pride myself on quality customer service, product knowledge and practical problem solving.
